MUSIC PRODUCTION

Music is life.

Life is pretty sweet.

Founded by Ben Crutchfield and Enda Gallen, we are passionate about working with passionate musicians and artists who want to achieve a distinct sonic vision for their work. We have a wide range of experience and taste, from alternative to pop, punk to electronic.

Most importantly we want to help artists realise what they want from their music and help them achieve this. We want to know you and build a relationship with you and your music, so that we can best serve your songs.
